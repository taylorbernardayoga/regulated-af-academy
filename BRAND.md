# Brand

## Who this is for

Self-aware women who spiral, shut down, or sabotage their relationships. They know their patterns. They can name them in therapy language. They just can't break them in the moment. They want to stop people-pleasing, stop abandoning themselves to keep the peace, and stop feeling privately exhausted while looking high-functioning on the outside.

## The transformation

She learns to catch herself in the triggered moment, regulate her body on the spot, heal the root wound (the part that learned "if I ask for too much, I'll be left"), and speak her needs plainly while turning her energy back toward herself.

She moves from anxious to secure. She becomes a fire, regulated baddie who walks into any room unafraid of what she says, trusting the right people will stay.

## The identity

The competent queen who says the thing and turns her energy back toward herself. Not smaller, not louder, just regulated.

## Core beliefs

- Real healing happens in the moment, not outside it.
- You can communicate without fighting or abandoning yourself.
- Your needs are not too much.
- Regulation is a skill, not a personality trait.

## The signature framework: the SECURE Method

- See it: catch the trigger as it happens
- Ease the charge: regulate the body on the spot
- Clear the root: heal the wound underneath the pattern
- Unlearn the pattern: break the old loop
- Relate new: practice the new way of relating
- Embody her: become the regulated version of yourself

## Voice

Warm, direct, grounded, bold. Sounds like her texting, not a copywriter. Spicy identity-naming copy that makes someone go "oh fuck, that's me." Swearing is on-brand. No corporate wellness speak, no toxic positivity, no clinical distance.

Never use em-dashes.

## Logo

No separate logo. The "Regulated AF" word treatment is the brand signature.

## The offer

Regulated AF Academy. 4-month 1:1 container. $2,200 or 4 payments of $597. 12 calls (3 per month, 50 minutes each) plus dripped education modules. The CTA is always: book a call.
